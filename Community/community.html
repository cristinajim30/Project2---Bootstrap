Our community

Handmade is a group of artisan and creative people who come together for a common good, to reduce our carbon footprint, using sustainable and eco-friendly materials.
In our shops you can find all the handmade products made by our community with love.
If you are a creative person and passionate about handmade things, you can join our community and we will be happy to welcome you. You can send us a form to the contact section with the subject: "I want to join".
